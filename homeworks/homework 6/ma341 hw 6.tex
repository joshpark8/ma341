    \section*{Exercise 3.3.3}
    Since $x_k\geq 2$,
    \[ x_{k+1} = 1 + \sqrt{x_k-1}\geq 1+\sqrt{2-1}=2\]
    So, $x_n\geq 2$ for all $n\in\N$ by induction. \\
    If $x_{k+1}\leq x_k$,
    \[x_{k+2}=1+\sqrt{x_{k+1}-1}\leq 1+\sqrt{x_k-1}=x_{k+1}\]
    So $\xn$ is decreasing. $x=1$ is not possible, so $\lim \xn = 2$
